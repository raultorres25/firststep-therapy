My Approach

“Life is a journey — and sometimes we need guidance to take the next step forward.”

I combine therapy, coaching and meditation — not just one, but a unique combination. We work on letting go of unresolved emotions, understanding how they affect you today, visualizing how you want to live, and putting it all into action.

I believe every person has the inner resources to grow — sometimes we just need guidance. Together we can open doors.

Meet Sonja

About Me

📜 Professional license in process · Cédula profesional en trámite

Sonja Skarstol

Psychotherapist · Coach · Meditation Instructor

I have learned through life that our experiences can become our greatest teachers. While my own journey has shaped me deeply, I know now I was never meant to do it alone — some helping hands showed up along the way. What began as a personal journey of healing and transformation soon became a calling to support others.

“Nothing ever goes away until it has taught us what we need to know.”
— Pema Chödrön

With over 25 years of experience living in Mexico, I have worked with individuals and groups from diverse backgrounds — in English and Spanish. I believe each person's cultural and spiritual beliefs are an essential part of their healing and should be honored.

You are your own master — but sometimes having someone with professional and personal experience helps. You are not alone. Your pain deserves a voice. Together we can change how you perceive and respond to the world, and that changes everything.
